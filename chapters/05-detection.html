What this means. The method is a structured evidence ladder from directly inspectable local structure to yearly interpretation.

The five-stage pipeline is designed to keep interpretation grounded. It starts with a yearly concept space, builds a filtration, extracts persistent H1 loops, reconstructs readable representative cycles, and then summarizes yearly boundary state [1][2]. Each stage adds context, but the claim boundary remains explicit.

This chapter is best read as "how outputs are produced." Local edges show concrete adjacency. Loop persistence shows unresolved boundary structure. Yearly summaries compare yearly states. Together they form one evidence ladder for discussion, not a deterministic prediction pipeline [3].
