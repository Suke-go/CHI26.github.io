What this means. The method is read as a progression from inspectable local evidence toward constrained yearly interpretation.

The five-stage pipeline is designed for continuity across scales rather than isolated outputs. It begins with yearly concept-space construction, then computes a filtration, extracts persistent H1 structure, reconstructs representative cycles for interpretability, and aggregates yearly descriptors for macro reading [1][2]. This sequencing keeps claims disciplined: local edges support concrete neighborhood inspection, meso cycles support boundary interpretation, and yearly summaries support comparative temporal reflection without asserting stable cross-year object identity. The method therefore produces a structured evidence ladder from what the output can show directly to what should remain interpretive and provisional [3].
