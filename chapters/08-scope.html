Important caution.

Scope is a trust-building device in this project, not a disclaimer added at the end. Each claim is tied to a measurable object and then bounded by what that object cannot establish, so readers can inspect where evidence is direct, where interpretation begins, and where conclusions should explicitly stop [1][2]. This chapter is designed to make those boundaries explicit enough for constructive disagreement without collapsing into either overclaiming or skepticism-by-default.
