8-1. What the analysis captures

  • Semantic proximity between concepts in year-specific embedding spaces
  • Community separation via Louvain partitioning
  • Boundary structures (persistent H₁ loops) and their robustness
  • Yearly topology descriptors (total persistence, triadic closure)
  • Ranked bridge candidates with interpretable representative cycles

8-2. What it does not establish

  • Institutional interdisciplinarity: semantic communities ≠ departments or funding streams
  • Causality: structural adjacency does not prove one field caused another to change
  • Author intent: the method reads aggregate corpus structure, not individual research decisions
  • Unique true path: the representative cycle is one witness, not the only valid decomposition
  • Aligned geometric tracking: each year's topology is independent; we do not track the “same” loop across years
  • Prospective prediction guarantee: validation is retrospective; deployment performance is not established
