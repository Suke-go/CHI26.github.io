08. What This Captures / What It Does Not

Trust in interpretation depends on clearly separating what the method can describe from what it cannot establish.

What this captures

  • Semantic adjacency patterns in the analyzed corpus.
  • Community structure in concept space.
  • Unresolved loop-like boundary structure.
  • Year-level structural summaries of boundary versus closure tendencies.

What this does not capture or establish

  • Causal explanation of why integration happened.
  • Institutional interdisciplinarity as a social fact.
  • Author intention or strategic collaboration motives.
  • A single true mechanism of integration.
  • Exact loop identity tracked continuously across years.

Yearly views compare independently computed summaries by year; they do not follow one persistent loop instance as an identity object through time.
