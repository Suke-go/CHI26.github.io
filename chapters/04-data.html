04. Data and Operationalization

This chapter defines what each unit means in the analysis pipeline so readers can interpret outputs consistently and avoid over-claiming.

Corpus

The analyzed yearly corpus is derived from major HCI venues including CHI, UIST, CSCW, and UbiComp.

Concepts

Concepts are noun-phrase-like units extracted from titles and abstracts, then embedded into a yearly semantic space.

Communities

Communities are data-driven semantic clusters in the concept graph, not fixed institutional subfields.

Cross-area links

In this project, a cross-area link does not mean institutional interdisciplinarity. It means two concepts from different semantic communities become structurally adjacent through intermediate concepts, even before a stable topic label is visible.

Operational caution: the method captures reorganization inside the analyzed corpus, not the full social or institutional history of HCI.
