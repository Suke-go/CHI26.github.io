In this project, topology means connectedness and holes, not exact geometric shape. A birth edge marks when a loop becomes topologically visible. Persistence means the loop remains unresolved for a range of thresholds. Yearly summaries then aggregate those signals by year, without claiming one loop is tracked as the same identity from year to year.

Minimal interpretation grammar

  • Birth: boundary condition emerges.
  • Persistence: unresolved separation remains.
  • Death/closure: local filling-in occurs, potentially indicating integration.

Representative cycles are explanatory reconstructions for reading structure; they are not one unique true pathway.
