Technical note (optional): why NDCG@50 and lead-time are reported

This note explains what the evaluation metrics are intended to capture in this site.

  • NDCG@50 weights high-rank correctness more than lower-rank correctness, so it matches the practical use case of inspecting top candidates first.
  • Lead-time metric captures how early a structural signal appears before later observable consolidation indicators.
  • Yearly metrics summarize each year independently; they are annual descriptors, not object-tracking trajectories of one loop instance.

What this means here: evaluation supports ranking utility and timing relevance, while interpretation still depends on transparent case reading and scope discipline.
