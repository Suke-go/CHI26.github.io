Important caution.

This chapter treats evidence as a layered argument rather than a single score. Micro, meso, and macro evidence are all required because they answer different validity questions: micro evidence answers "is the structure interpretable in concrete terms?"; meso evidence answers "does the structure improve comparative candidate ordering?"; macro evidence answers "what yearly structural condition is becoming more or less common?" [1][2]. Removing any one level weakens the discussion foundation: without micro evidence, outputs are hard to inspect; without meso evidence, comparative utility is unclear; without macro evidence, field-level context is under-specified.

Real case: VR–Therapy representative cycle

Representative cycle sequence:

The VR–Therapy cycle is valuable because it makes a boundary pattern auditable in plain terms. The cycle indicates repeated adjacency among immersion, exergame, and therapy-related concepts before complete closure, supporting the claim that a boundary condition can be visible before a single dominant topic label fully consolidates. At the same time, this case should not be over-read as proof of a unique disciplinary merger pathway; it is one interpretable witness of structural reorganization, intended to ground discussion rather than close it.

Real metric block

Ranking evidence supports a narrower claim than prediction narratives often imply. Improved ranking scores show that topology-informed features help prioritize plausible bridge candidates relative to baselines in this evaluation setting; they do not establish that the system predicts future field history with certainty, nor that the highest-ranked items are causally privileged. In other words, ranking evidence supports comparative ordering utility under defined conditions, not deterministic foresight [3].

Macro interpretation note. Read yearly topology-derived summaries as annual structural weather: they indicate prevailing openness or closure pressure, not the persistent identity of one loop across years.

Interpretive use

Pair annual persistent H1 totals with closure tendency to discuss changing structural conditions at field scale. The macro view is strongest when interpreted together with inspectable micro cycles and meso ranking comparisons, so field-level statements remain anchored to concrete evidence objects.
