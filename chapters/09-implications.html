What this means. Calm interpretability can inform decisions without turning this site into a prediction dashboard.

The practical implication is to add a disciplined structural lens to existing HCI and science-of-science methods. Representative cycles support qualitative follow-up, yearly summaries support reflective planning, and ranking evidence supports candidate prioritization [1][2].

Three discussion anchors

What is being detected

The main object is a persistent H1 loop in yearly concept space, interpreted as an unresolved boundary state.

What counts as evidence

Evidence is strongest when cycle-level reading, ranking metrics, and yearly summaries are used together.

What remains open for discussion

Interpretation still needs judgment when moving from structural signal to field-level meaning.

Discussion agenda

This project opens a focused research discussion rather than a generic prompt list.

  1. What should count as meaningful reorganization in HCI? Distinguish temporary adjacency from stable restructuring with loop persistence, closure patterns, and domain interpretation.
  2. How much interpretability is enough? Define minimum readability standards for cycle explanation, metric transparency, and scope notes before method claims are accepted.
  3. How should evidence scales be combined? Use local examples, ranking numbers, and yearly summaries so they reinforce each other.
  4. When does a boundary become a field? Specify what additional qualitative and institutional evidence is required beyond topology outputs.
