03. Existing Approaches

This project is positioned as a complement to existing Science of Science and HCI-mapping approaches, not a replacement.

Citation-based approaches

Strong for lineage, influence, and knowledge flow. Weaker for early structural adjacency before citation patterns stabilize.

Co-word approaches

Strong for visible topic distributions and vocabulary trends. Weaker for boundary states that exist before repeated explicit co-occurrence.

Semantic approaches

Strong for local similarity in embedding space. Weaker for interpreting unfilled structural relations beyond nearest-neighbor proximity.

Graph prediction approaches

Strong for likely closure under existing network structure. Weaker for explaining why a boundary exists as an interpretable unresolved condition.

What this project adds: unresolved loop structure as a readable boundary signal before direct integration becomes obvious.
