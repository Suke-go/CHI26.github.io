D. Data and temporal construction

  • Corpus scope: CHI, UIST, CSCW, and UbiComp.
  • Time span: 1999–2025 in the current project materials, with CSCW available through 2024.
  • Concept extraction: noun-phrase level concepts from titles/abstracts, followed by project-defined filtering.
  • Representation logic: year-specific concept representation and distance structure are recomputed for each year.
  • Temporal honesty rule: year t uses only records up to t; no future leakage into construction.

Constructing each year from history-only data ensures that temporal validation is meaningful: candidates from year t are generated without access to future years used for evaluation.
