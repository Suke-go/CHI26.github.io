Important caution.

Rather than framing methods as competitors, this chapter positions them as temporally and analytically complementary lenses. Citation and co-word approaches are excellent once a topic’s language and influence patterns stabilize, while semantic and graph methods support local proximity and ranking decisions in evolving neighborhoods [1][2][3]. Topological summaries add value by making unresolved meso-structure interpretable during earlier boundary conditions, where labels remain fluid and ties are indirect [4]. The intended continuity is practical: use bibliometric baselines to situate maturity, use local semantic evidence to inspect candidate links, and use persistent loops to characterize what remains structurally open at each year [4].
