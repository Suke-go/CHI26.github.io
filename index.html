CHI 2026 Supplement


This visual essay explains how yearly topology summaries can show boundary reorganization in HCI before a new area appears as one stable topic label. The corpus covers about 30,000 papers across CHI, UIST, CSCW, and UbiComp from 1999–2025 (CSCW through 2024).

Site Promise

  • What this site explains: how yearly concept-space topology can surface boundary states and bridge candidates.
  • What you will learn: how to read local loops, ranking evidence, and yearly summaries together.
  • How to read claims: clear claim boundaries are explained in Chapter 8.
